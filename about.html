How It Started

We Lived the Problem
Before We Solved It

Most painting companies work 9 to 5, take a week to quote, and leave your home in disarray. We saw this first-hand — as customers, as contractors, as people who believed there had to be a smarter way.

So we built 25/7 Painting from scratch. Not as a traditional painting company, but as a precision service operation. Virtual quoting came first — because why should you have to clear your schedule for an estimator to come over when technology makes it unnecessary?

Then came the scheduling system, the clean-room protocol, the final walkthrough guarantee. Every piece of the experience was engineered to be better than what existed. That's why we call it the 25/7 Standard — there's always an extra hour in the day when you're built to be efficient.

The Values Behind
Every Job We Do

🎯

Precision Over Speed

We're fast — but never at the expense of quality. Every cut, every coat, every edge is done the right way, every time. We don't cut corners because our clients are counting on us not to.

🤝

Honest & Transparent

No hidden fees, no surprise charges, no upselling. Your quote is your price. We communicate clearly at every step and tell you exactly what we're doing and why.

🧹

The Clean-Room Standard

We deep-clean after every job — no exceptions. You should not be able to tell a crew was in your home except for the perfect, fresh paint on your walls.

💡

Always Innovating

Virtual quoting, precision scheduling, digital walkthroughs — we continuously invest in systems that make the experience better for our clients, not just easier for us.
